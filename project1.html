Green Tea

Green tea has been cherished for centuries across Asia and is known for its light, grassy flavor and vibrant color. From ancient Chinese dynasties to modern health trends, green tea continues to be a symbol of calm, mindfulness, and wellness.

Harvested early and quickly heated to prevent oxidation, green tea retains its natural antioxidants. Different regions have their own signature styles, like Japan’s matcha or China’s Dragon Well.
